Copyediting and Proofreading Bring Out the Best in Your Words

Reader understanding and enjoyment are enhanced when text flows smoothly. Clear and concise written communications make a strong impact, providing maximum benefit to readers and you, your company, or your organization.

Copyediting and proofreading by Fred dissolve the constant worry about possible text shortcomings and having to apologize for them. Such mistakes as misspelled words and poor grammar are not only embarrassing but costly, since time and money are wasted on redoing materials. Draw upon Fred's copyediting and proofreading expertise and experience.

Because of his years as an editor, as Fred copyedits or proofreads he can analyze the content for writer intent and reader comprehension and provide insightful feedback.

To assure your text is high quality, Fred looks for such details as:

  • Faulty spelling, grammar, and punctuation
  • Consistency in spelling, hyphenation, numerals, fonts, and capitalization
  • Incorrect usage
  • Specific references
  • Sequencing (such as alphabetical order) in lists and other material
  • Smooth transitions
  • Missing text
  • Parallel structure in text and headings
  • Consistent handling of key terms
  • Ambiguous or incorrect statements
  • Wordiness, triteness, and inappropriate jargon
  • Relocation of sentences
  • Additions and deletions
