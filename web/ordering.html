Presentation

All photographs are crafted by Fred in his darkroom. Each image is produced on fiber silver gelatin paper. Fiber is an art paper of the highest quality, producing a rich-looking image.

The available sizes of photographs are 4x5, 5x7, 8x10, 11x14, and 16x20 inches. Each photograph has approximately a quarter-inch border. Cropping of a photograph as displayed on this site may be required according to the proportions of the requested size.

Only fiber photos are available matted or framed. The white window mat covers approximately an eighth-inch on each side of the photo. Matted sizes (vertical and horizontal) are: 8x10 (for a 4x5 photograph), 11x14 (for a 5x7), 14x18 (for an 8x10), 16x20 (for an 11x14), and 22x28 (for a 16x20). The horizontal framed sizes are: 9x9½ (for a 4x5 photograph), 11x12½ (for a 5x7), 14x15½ (for an 8x10), 18x20½ (for an 11x14), and 24x27½ (for a 16x20). The vertical framed sizes are: 8½x10 (for a 4x5 photograph), 10½x13 (for a 5x7), 13½x16 (for an 8x10), 17½x21 (for an 11x14), and 23½x28 (for a 16x20).

All mounting and backing materials are archival quality, meaning they are free of acids that deteriorate a photograph. The materials will last as long as the photograph (75 years or longer), and the brightness and tone of the mat board will not diminish. Each flat-black anodized aluminum frame is of museum-grade quality. The finished framed photograph appears similar to the way the photos are displayed on the Web site.

Every photograph is signed and comes with an artist's statement and a label containing the title, scene location, date when the scene was photographed, and signature. The label may be affixed to the back of the framed work if you so desire.
